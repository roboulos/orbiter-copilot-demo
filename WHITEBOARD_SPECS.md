• Glossy white whiteboard surface
• Dry-erase markers: blue (titles), black (body), red (warnings), green (highlights)
• Hand-drawn imperfections (slightly irregular letters, wavy lines)
• Photographed at 10-15 degree angle (not perfectly straight-on)
• Office lighting (natural or fluorescent)
• Realistic marker stroke thickness variation
• Some minor smudging acceptable

**Drawing Style:**
• Boxes have hand-drawn rounded corners
• Arrows are simple with basic arrowheads
• Text size varies (titles large, details smaller)
• Consistent handwriting across all 4 boards
• Blue for structure, black for content
• Lines connecting elements show flow/relationships

**Composition:**
• All 4 should feel like same whiteboard session
• Similar marker types and handwriting
• Similar level of detail and density
• Consistent photo angle and lighting
• Readable at standard whiteboard viewing distance
